About Sunny Innovation Lab

Building games and apps with global teams, from idea to launch, leveraging both human creativity and AI-powered vibe coding.

Sunny Innovation Lab is a voluntary group of developers, UI/UX designers, artists, and game/app designers representing K-PADA, a premier community of Korean Programmers, Artists, Designers & Architects based in Canada and South Korea.

Together, we learn, build, and create apps and games that make a difference. Our mission is to deliver useful, enjoyable, and meaningful experiences. If our users feel happier, more inspired, or simply have a little more fun while using our products, then we have achieved our goal.
