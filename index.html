About Sunny Innovation Lab

A voluntary global team creating games and apps.

Sunny Innovation Lab is a voluntary group of developers, artists, and game designers from Canada, the United States, Japan, and South Korea who work together to create games and apps.

Our internal goal is to learn through hands-on development, collaborate throughout the process, and grow by going through a full development cycle from MVP to launch.

Our external goal is to provide feasible, useful, fun, and valuable products to users.
